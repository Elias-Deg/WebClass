Daily Teaching Flow

  1. Ask what they remember from the previous session.
  2. Explain the new concept with a simple analogy.
  3. Watch or assign the recommended video section.
  4. Demo the smallest useful example.
  5. Let the learner build a similar version.
  6. Assign homework and a research question.
  7. End with checkpoint questions.

Mentor Rule

Do not move forward just because the calendar says so. Move forward when they can explain the concept in plain language and build a tiny working example.
